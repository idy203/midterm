A Look Into the Tarot of Persona 5
Persona 5 is a turn based Japanese role-playing game developed by Atlus. The tarot system within the game is represented by specific characters connecting with a certain tarot in regards to personality traits and abilities, which further connects with Personas. Personas are manifestations of the willpower someone has, mainly against hardships or wrongdoings.
